Precaution
* Soil-test-based balanced nutrient management should strictly be followed to maintain the healthy status.
* Wet conditions promote disease, so water trees at the base and be careful not to splash water on leaves.
* Avoid dry spots, overfertilizing with nitrogen, and maintain as high a cutting height as possible.
